Pre-Game

Role Assignments

  • Player A — Jet Gun Handler

    Heats the Pylon, holds the Jet Gun, leads the explosive-kill step.

  • Player B — "The Ears" (Stuhlinger)

    Only character who hears Richtofen. Calls out every step confirmation. Keeps a zombie alive between steps.

  • Player C — Parts Runner

    Runs parts collection in parallel so the crew never waits — grabs fog pieces while the bus rolls on.

  • Player D — Floater / Reviver

    Handles crowd control, revives during steps, and covers whichever lane is thinnest. Fourth lamp on the EMP step.

Rough income — per-player cumulative points if you're playing well in a 4-player game. Estimate only; reviving or setting up the EE earns less, so treat as a sanity check, not a guarantee.

Best Gun: M16 → Skullcrusher

Cheap wall buy at the Bus Depot (no box gamble), and Pack-a-Punched it becomes a 3-round-burst beast with an underbarrel grenade launcher — those grenades double as Ray Gun backup for the explosive Pylon step. Honorable mentions: MP5 → MP58 for fast training; keep the Ray Gun UN-upgraded — the ordinary Ray Gun counts as explosive for the kill step.

Every-Round Habits

Train zombies in loops; don't fight them scattered  ·  Bank-bonus early rounds: shoot zombies a few times before knifing for max points  ·  Player B calls out every Richtofen voice cue  ·  Players C and D revive and cover crowd control during steps. No dog rounds / no guaranteed Max Ammo in TranZit — refills come from random drops only.
